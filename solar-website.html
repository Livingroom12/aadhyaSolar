About Us

We're Aadhya Solar

Aadhya Solar is India's fastest-growing solar panel manufacturer, delivering innovative clean energy solutions since 2015. With a production capacity of 12 GW using the latest TOPCon technology, we've earned the trust of over 5,000 customers globally.

Technology

Why Our Tech Leads

We invest heavily in next-generation solar technology to deliver the highest efficiency and durability to our customers.

01
TOPCon Cell Technology
Tunnel Oxide Passivated Contact technology reduces energy loss at cell surfaces, delivering industry-leading 23.5% efficiency.
02
Half-Cut Cell Design
Cells cut in half reduce resistance losses and improve shading tolerance — generating more power in real conditions.
03
Anti-PID Technology
Anti-Potential Induced Degradation ensures long-term performance stability, protecting your investment for 25+ years.
04
Low Light Performance
Advanced cell architecture ensures excellent output even in cloudy conditions and at dawn/dusk.
